Accepted Payment Methods

Bank Deposit

Parents may pay through the school's official bank account. Details are available from the bursar.

Mobile Money

Where available, mobile money payment details will be provided by the school administration.

School Cash Office

Payments can be made directly at the school bursar's office during official working hours.

Student Requirements

Uniform

Official school uniform and sportswear.

Learning Materials

Exercise books, pens, mathematical set and textbooks.

Boarding Items

Bedding, mosquito net, toiletries and personal effects.

Important Notice

Only the official fees approved by the school administration should be paid. Always obtain an official receipt after payment. For updated fee information, please contact the school bursar.
