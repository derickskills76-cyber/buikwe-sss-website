About Buikwe Senior Secondary School

Discover our history, vision, mission and commitment to excellence.

Who We Are

Buikwe Senior Secondary School is a mixed day and boarding secondary school located in Buikwe District, Uganda. The school is committed to providing quality education, discipline, leadership skills and preparing learners to meet the challenges of the modern world.

Through dedicated teachers, supportive administration and a conducive learning environment, the school continues to promote academic excellence and character development.

Our History

Buikwe Senior Secondary School was established to provide accessible and quality secondary education to learners in Buikwe District and surrounding communities. The school has grown into an institution that focuses on academic achievement, discipline, innovation and preparing students for further education and responsible citizenship.
