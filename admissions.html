Admissions

Start your journey towards academic excellence at Buikwe S.S.

Welcome To Buikwe S.S.S Admissions

Buikwe Senior Secondary School welcomes students who are ready to learn, grow and develop their talents in a supportive academic environment.

Our admission process is designed to provide equal opportunities to learners while maintaining high standards of discipline and academic commitment.

Why Choose Buikwe S.S.S?

Quality Education

Experienced teachers and a strong academic foundation for learners.

Conducive Environment

A safe and supportive environment for learning and growth.

Talent Development

Sports, leadership and co-curricular activities.

Admission Requirements

Academic Documents

- Previous school report forms
- UNEB results where applicable
- Transfer documents for continuing students

Personal Documents

- Passport photographs
- Birth certificate copy
- Parent/guardian details

Health Requirements

- Medical information
- Emergency contacts

Admission Process

Step 1

Obtain admission information from the school office.

Step 2

Submit required documents and complete registration.

Step 3

Receive joining instructions and report to school.

Day And Boarding Education

Buikwe Senior Secondary School provides a learning environment that supports both day and boarding students. Learners receive academic guidance, discipline and care throughout their school journey.
