Our Academic Programmes

At Buikwe Senior Secondary School, academics are at the heart of our mission. We provide a supportive learning environment where students develop knowledge, creativity, critical thinking and practical skills.

Our academic programmes follow the Uganda National Curriculum and are designed to prepare learners for higher education and professional careers.

Levels Of Education

Ordinary Level (S1-S4)

Students are equipped with strong foundations in sciences, languages, humanities and technical subjects.

Advanced Level (S5-S6)

Learners specialise in different combinations that prepare them for university and careers.

ICT Education

Students gain computer skills required in the modern digital world.

Curriculum Areas

Sciences

Physics, Chemistry and Biology.

Humanities

History, Geography, Religious Studies and Economics.

Languages

English, Literature and local languages.

Technical Subjects

Computer Studies, Agriculture and vocational skills.

Academic Assessment

Students are continuously assessed through classroom tests, internal examinations and national examinations including UNEB assessments.
